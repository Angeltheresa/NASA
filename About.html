About Green Sensus

Welcome to the Green Sensus application! Our goal is to provide you with the tools and resources you need to maintain a healthy and sustainable garden. From weed control to climate data, we offer a variety of features designed to help you make informed decisions about your gardening practices.

Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting out, our user-friendly interface and comprehensive calculators will assist you in optimizing your garden's health and productivity.
